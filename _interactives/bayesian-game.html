The Bayesian Diagnostic Game

A 68-year-old man arrives in your ED with shortness of breath. Work through the clinical encounter and watch diagnosis probabilities shift in real time.

At each stage, make the clinical choices you think fit this patient. Every finding applies likelihood ratios that update the differential through Bayes' theorem.
